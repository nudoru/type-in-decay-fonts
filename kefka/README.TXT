Kefka by Matt Perkins.

Inspired by some old ansi's laying around on my hard drive and other stuff.
Kinda like midieval grafiti, eh?
There are no numbers but you can do Roman Numerals!!! >:)

1 = I
2 = II
3 = III
4 = V
5 = X
6 = L
7 = C
8 = M
